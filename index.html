Labor rate prices displayed are self-reported by collision repair centers. Price is not the only factor to consider when selecting a collision repair center. Customers may also consider their type of vehicle and the shop's training, equipment, and certifications as other criteria. Customers can confirm labor prices and other criteria with their collision center.

*NABR verifies collision certifications annually, provided that OEMs publish their certified shop lists publicly. Labor prices may be higher for these body shops to account for their investment required to achieve certification. Customers can confirm manufacturer collision certifications through the Certified1Stop.com portal.
